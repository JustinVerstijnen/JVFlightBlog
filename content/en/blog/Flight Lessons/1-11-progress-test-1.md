> Lesson 1.11 – Progress test 1 Dates: 20-07-2026 & 04-08-2026

## Progress test 1 (20-07-2026)

Another lesson today after again one cancelled lesson because of the weather. Flying with 34 degrees celcius was not what we wanted. Haha.

Today we had around 20 degrees celcius, with a 8 knots wind from the north (variable between 280 and 040) and a sky full of cumulus clouds, starting at 2800 feet AGL. A great day for some VFR flying with no significant weather or other risks.

So today we will do the progress test for phase one. A lesson of one hour to perform every exercise done in phase one:

- Steep turns with 45 degrees bank angle
- Stalls in clean, approach and landing configuration
- Climbing and descending turns
- Two circuits
- Landing the plane with some crosswind (~8 knots)

## Homework

I got some homework for the next lesson, which will also be a progress test but then a final test for both the exercises above and the circuits.
